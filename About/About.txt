About Bazaars
What Is Bazaars (BZR)?
Bazaars is a next-generation peer-to-peer marketplace revolutionizing global commerce through the privacy, security, and transparency of blockchain technology. Designed for buyers and sellers to seamlessly exchange goods using cryptocurrency, Bazaars bridges the gap between traditional trade and the decentralized economy. With a focus on user empowerment, accessibility, and trust, Bazaars is shaping a future where financial freedom and inclusivity will transform how the world connects and conducts business. The Bazaars.app marketplace is live with decentralized payments and the BZR token.

How Many BZR tokens are there in circulation?
BZR was deployed on the Ethereum main network on April 18, 2022, with 555,555,555 BZR tokens created at genesis. There are currently 11.67M BZR tokens in circulation.
Who are the founders of Bazaars?
Bazaars was founded by Cryptomoe who explained in his white paper to core developers that cryptocurrency and blockchain technology could benefit from peer-to-peer marketplaces and the technology could allow physical and digital assets to be swapped simultaneously.
What is Bazaars?
Bazaars represents an innovative approach to e-commerce, leveraging the power of blockchain technology to facilitate a decentralized peer-to-peer (P2P) marketplace. This platform allows for the exchange of both physical and digital assets, making it a versatile tool for users worldwide. The initiative is spearheaded by Bazaars Limited, with its foundations in the British Virgin Islands, emphasizing the global reach and ambition of the project.

At the heart of Bazaars' ecosystem is the BZR token, an ERC20 token that operates on the Ethereum blockchain. This token not only facilitates transactions within the Bazaars marketplace but also serves as a potential means for token holders to earn a percentage of the profits generated from the marketplace's operations. The introduction of the Bazaars Blockchain and Bazaars Wallet is underway, aiming to further enhance the platform's functionality and user experience.

The Bazaars app, accessible on major mobile platforms, underscores the project's commitment to accessibility and user engagement. It offers a wide array of features, including free listings for high-value items such as properties and vehicles, a direct line of communication between buyers and sellers, and a robust moderation system to ensure a secure and seamless trading environment. Additionally, the platform supports cryptocurrency transactions, facilitated through a secure payment gateway, to streamline the buying and selling process.

As of its deployment on Ethereum on April 18, 2022, there were over half a billion BZR tokens created, with a significant portion already in circulation. The distribution strategy includes a public sale on Uniswap, with a portion of the tokens locked for future sales and reserves allocated for development and operational purposes.

The vision behind Bazaars, as articulated by its founder Cryptomoe, is to harness cryptocurrency and blockchain technology to revolutionize peer-to-peer marketplaces. By enabling the simultaneous swapping of physical and digital assets, Bazaars aims to address the evolving needs of the global e-commerce landscape.
